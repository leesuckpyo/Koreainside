Real travel problems

The best transfer depends on what can go wrong

Airport Transfer should answer practical arrival problems before users leave the terminal.

Late-night arrival

Best check: Last train, night bus and taxi availability.

Too many suitcases

Best check: Walking distance, stairs and taxi size.

Family travelers

Best check: Few transfers, short walking and easy loading.

Missed last train

Best check: Night bus, taxi or pickup backup.

Long walking distance

Best check: Airport bus stop, taxi drop-off and hotel entrance.

FAQ

Airport Transfer FAQ

Should I take AREX or Airport Bus?

AREX is usually better for speed and rail-connected areas. Airport Bus is often better when your hotel is close to a bus stop or you want fewer luggage transfers.

Can I use a taxi with four large suitcases?

A regular taxi may not be suitable for four travelers with large suitcases. A large taxi or Airport Pickup may be more practical.

Is the Night Bus safe?

Night Bus can be useful after midnight, but users should check route availability and stop location before relying on it.

How much does a taxi cost?

Taxi cost depends on destination, time, traffic, taxi type and surcharges.

Do I need Airport Pickup?

Airport Pickup is useful for families, large groups, late-night arrivals, heavy luggage or hotels that are difficult to reach by public transportation.
