Why this guide exists

Korea works differently than you expect.

Travelers in Korea often get stuck on how things actually work, not on which landmark to visit. Airport arrival, transportation cards, eSIM setup, payments, maps and booking habits can all be different from home. KR Inside helps foreign visitors compare options, understand limits and make better decisions in Korea.
