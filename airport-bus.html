How to Use Airport Bus

Step-by-step guide to finding airport bus routes, buying tickets and choosing airport bus instead of AREX or taxi.

Quick answer

Airport buses are often easier than trains when your hotel is near a bus stop or when you have heavy luggage.

When should I use the airport bus?

Choose the airport bus if your destination is far from Seoul Station, if your hotel is close to an airport bus stop, or if you want fewer transfers with luggage.

What to do

  1. After exiting arrivals, follow signs for Airport Bus.
  2. Check your destination or hotel area on the route board.
  3. Buy a ticket at a staffed counter or ticket machine.
  4. Confirm the bus number, platform and last bus time.

Common mistakes

  • Choosing a bus only by district name without checking the exact stop.
  • Not checking the last bus time after late-night arrival.
  • Putting luggage on the wrong bus without confirming the destination.

FAQ

Is airport bus better than AREX?

It depends on your hotel location. AREX is predictable, but airport buses can be easier for hotels away from subway stations.

Can I pay with a foreign card?

Some counters and machines may accept foreign cards, but prepare a backup payment method.

Can I take large luggage?

Yes. Airport buses are usually convenient for large luggage.
