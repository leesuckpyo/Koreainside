Shade testing and personal color

Makeup & personal color

Use the color analysis as a guide, then test the final shade in natural light.

What you actually do
Test base and color products, or attend a scheduled personal color session with recommendations.
Why travelers choose it

Best for

Hair & scalp care

Check whether you are booking styling, routine scalp care, or a consultation about a specific concern.

What you actually do
Buy hair products, book a cut or styling service, or discuss scalp concerns with an appropriate provider.
Why travelers choose it
A salon visit can fit into a travel day, but it still takes time and may require an appointment.
Best for
Travelers who know whether their priority is appearance, maintenance or a professional opinion.
Watch out

Decision: The better provider is the one whose stated service matches the outcome you are actually seeking.

02 · Choose the area

Where to explore K-Beauty in Seoul

Pick the district based on the shops, appointments, and other plans you want to combine. Myeongdong favors an efficient first visit, Seongsu and Hongdae suit trend-led browsing, and Gangnam or Apgujeong & Cheongdam are more relevant to scheduled premium services.

Myeongdong

Many stores within a short walk

Area character
High-volume central shopping with many product categories close together.
Good for
First visits, efficient browsing and combining beauty with central sightseeing.
Advantages
Easy navigation, broad selection and convenient transport.
Watch out
Crowds and promotion-heavy displays can encourage rushed purchases.
Stay nearby
Useful for short trips or repeated central shopping days.

Decision: Myeongdong fits trips where convenience matters more than a quiet, curated visit.

03 · Plan the next purchase

Buy in Korea or reorder later?

Compare the full cost, including delivery, taxes, import charges, and possible returns. Availability, delivery, taxes and returns can change the practical choice.

During the trip

Buy during your Korea trip

  • Availability: Broadest access to local ranges
  • Delivery: Immediate
  • Shipping: Luggage space and airline limits matter
  • Taxes: Check current visitor and customs rules
  • Returns: Difficult after departure
  • Convenience: High when the item is already tested
At home

Reorder from an official store in your country

  • Availability: Smaller local catalog
  • Delivery: Usually faster domestically
  • Shipping: Often simpler to understand
  • Taxes: Commonly included in local pricing
  • Returns: Local policy may be easier to use
  • Convenience: Highest for routine repeat purchases
International

Order internationally from Korea

  • Availability: May include Korea-only products
  • Delivery: Longer and less predictable
  • Shipping: Can outweigh a product-price difference
  • Taxes: Import charges may apply
  • Returns: Often costly or impractical
  • Convenience: Useful for items unavailable locally

Practical answers

Common K-Beauty planning questions

Where should first-time visitors shop?

Myeongdong is the easiest starting point for broad retail comparison and central transport. Set a category or spending limit before entering promotion-heavy stores.

Do beauty clinics require reservations?

Many consultations and procedures do, especially when an interpreter or specific clinician is requested. Confirm the appointment, consultation process, total price, risks and aftercare directly with the provider.

Is personal color analysis available in English?

Some studios offer English sessions or interpretation, but availability varies. Verify the working language, session length, report format and cancellation policy before booking.

Are products cheaper in Korea?

Some products may have favorable local pricing or sets, while others do not. Compare the exact size and formula, then include luggage, shipping, tax and return limitations.

Can I reorder products after returning home?

Often, but the same product may use a different name, package or formula in another market. Save the full label and check an official local or international channel before buying multiples.

How should I compare official stores and marketplaces?

Check seller identity, product origin, return terms and whether the manufacturer recognizes the sales channel. A lower price is less useful when authenticity or support is unclear.

What should sensitive-skin travelers check?

Review ingredients and instructions, patch test where appropriate, and avoid introducing several unfamiliar products together. Seek qualified medical advice for active conditions or previous serious reactions.

Which Seoul area is best for a K-Beauty-focused trip?

Myeongdong favors easy retail, Seongsu favors trends, Hongdae combines casual shopping with nightlife, and Gangnam or Apgujeong suit scheduled premium services. Choose a hotel nearby only when the area supports several plans.

Choose a base after you know how many appointments, shopping days and late finishes your plan includes. Choose a base that keeps your planned shops, appointments, and evening activities within a manageable route.
