Choose by destination, arrival time, luggage, group size and how many transfers you can manage. Check current operating information before you travel.

AREX

Fits: Travelers heading toward Seoul Station, Hongdae or another rail-connected destination.

Watch out: Your accommodation may still require a subway, bus or taxi transfer.

Airport Bus

Fits: Travelers staying near a bus stop or carrying luggage they do not want to move through stations.

Watch out: Confirm the route and current timetable, and allow for road traffic.

Keep the information you may need even if your internet or payment method stops working.

An English address may be difficult to use.
Save the accommodation name, phone number and address in Korean.
Internet setup can fail or take time.

Problems after arrival

Solve Problems in the Public Arrival Hall

These are the problems that can block your next step after immigration, baggage claim and customs are complete.

My internet connection failed

Problem: Your eSIM, roaming or mobile data does not connect after arrival.

What to do now: Use airport Wi-Fi to check your provider's activation instructions, phone settings and support contact before leaving.

Backup option: Use your offline screenshots until you can contact the provider or choose another connection method.

Review the eSIM guide →

My foreign card was declined

Problem: Your credit or debit card does not work at an airport store, kiosk or machine.

What to do now: Try a staffed counter or a different card, and check whether international transactions are enabled.

Backup option: Use another card or cash rather than relying on one payment method.

Review the payments guide →

I cannot find AREX, the bus or a taxi

Problem: You are in the arrival hall but cannot find the correct transport entrance, stop or stand.

What to do now: Confirm your terminal, use the official airport map and follow the current airport signs for your chosen transport.

Backup option: Ask an information desk to point out the correct route instead of following an unofficial offer.

Compare airport transport →

I arrived late at night

Problem: Depending on your arrival time, AREX or regular airport buses may have finished operating.

What to do now: Check the official timetable because some late-night bus services may still operate from your terminal.

Backup option: Use an official taxi stand or follow the meeting instructions for a pre-booked pickup.

Check the airport bus guide →

The driver cannot identify my accommodation

Problem: The English name or address does not clearly identify your destination.

What to do now: Show the Korean-language address and phone number, then open the saved location in a Korean map app.

Backup option: Use your booking screenshot or contact the accommodation through airport Wi-Fi.

Review the maps guide →

FAQ

Incheon Airport First 30 Minutes FAQ

Short answers for decisions you need to make in the public arrival hall.

What should I do first after entering the arrival hall?

Check your internet connection, save your accommodation address in Korean, confirm that you have a working payment method and choose transport for your destination.

Do I need to buy T-money immediately?

No. T-money is useful when you plan to use buses or subways, but a traveler taking a taxi, pre-booked transfer or rental car does not need to treat it as the first required purchase.

How do I choose between AREX, airport bus and taxi?

Use your destination, arrival time, luggage, group size and tolerance for transfers. AREX suits rail-connected destinations, buses can reduce station transfers, and taxis provide door-to-door travel.

What should I do after a late-night arrival?

Check the current official timetable. AREX or regular buses may have finished, but some late-night buses may still operate. Otherwise, use an official taxi stand or your pre-booked pickup instructions.

Where can I check my terminal and airport facilities?

Check the official arrival flight page for your terminal and use the official airport map for facilities. If you must change terminals in the public area, use the free terminal shuttle bus or the paid Airport Railroad and confirm current operating details. The airside shuttle train is for transfer routes, not ordinary public-area terminal travel.

What information should I save before leaving the airport?

Save your Korean accommodation address, phone number, booking confirmation, transport plan, important QR codes and a support contact for your mobile data provider.
