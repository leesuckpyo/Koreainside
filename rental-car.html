How to Rent a Car in Korea

Practical guide to deciding whether a rental car makes sense in Korea, especially for regional travel outside Seoul.

Quick answer

Rental cars are usually not needed for Seoul-only travel. They make more sense for regional trips, long stays or road trips outside Seoul.

When does a rental car make sense?

Consider renting a car if you plan to visit multiple cities, rural areas or places with limited public transport. For Seoul, public transport and taxis are usually easier.

What to check before booking

  1. International driving permit requirements.
  2. Insurance coverage and deductible.
  3. Pickup and return location.
  4. Parking conditions at your hotel.
  5. Toll road and navigation setup.

Common mistakes

  • Renting a car for Seoul city travel only.
  • Not checking parking availability.
  • Assuming every foreign license is accepted without an international driving permit.

FAQ

Should I rent a car at Incheon Airport?

Only if your trip needs it. For Seoul, AREX, airport bus and taxi are usually easier.

Is driving in Seoul easy?

Not for first-time visitors. Traffic, parking and navigation can be stressful.

What is the best alternative?

Use AREX, airport bus or taxi for Seoul. Rent a car only for regional travel.
