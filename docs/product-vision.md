# Korea Inside Product Vision

## Mission

Help travelers understand how Korea really works.

---

## Vision

Become the world's most trusted guide
for understanding Korea before and during travel.

---

## Core Principles

### 1.

Solve real travel problems.

Do not create content just to increase page count.

### 2.

Trust is more important than monetization.

### 3.

Help users make their own decisions.

Never push products.

### 4.

Show before explaining.

Images first.

Text second.

### 5.

Everything should answer

"Oh, now I understand."

### 6.

Every page must connect naturally to another page.

Build journeys, not isolated pages.

### 7.

Quality over quantity.

Complete pages before creating new ones.

### 8.

Use AI to improve efficiency,

not to replace accuracy.

### 9.

Always design for global users,

while explaining Korea correctly.

### 10.

Keep improving using real user behavior.

---

## Product Goals

- High trust
- High usability
- High SEO quality
- High accessibility
- Long-term maintainability

---

## Non Goals

Korea Inside is NOT

- a travel agency
- a hotel booking site
- a tourist attraction catalog
- an advertisement platform
- a clickbait media site

---

## Success Definition

A successful page is one where users

understand Korea better,

stay longer,

visit related pages,

and leave with confidence.
