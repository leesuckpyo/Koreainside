# eSIM Decision Flow Infographic

Asset: `images/infographics/esim-decision-flow.svg`

Date: 2026-07-01

## Purpose

This SVG infographic helps Korea travelers decide whether they should use a data-only eSIM, a Korean number eSIM, a physical SIM card, or roaming.

It is designed for decision support, not sales. The flow emphasizes phone compatibility, Korean phone number needs, and identity verification limits.

## Usage

Use this asset near eSIM decision-making content, especially sections explaining:

- Phone eSIM compatibility
- Data-only eSIM vs Korean number eSIM
- Physical SIM or roaming alternatives
- Korean identity verification warnings
- Provider checklist before purchase
