What is eSIM?

An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. You install a mobile plan by scanning a QR code or entering an activation code instead of inserting a physical SIM card.

Why it helps in Korea

  • Prepare internet before landing
  • No need to change your physical SIM
  • Keep your home SIM active on supported phones
  • Use maps, messaging, translation and web search after arrival

Important difference

Not all eSIM plans include the same features. Some are data-only. Some Korean eSIM plans may include a Korean phone number, voice calls and SMS.

Common eSIM Problems

Most issues come from activation timing, wrong SIM line settings, disabled data roaming or provider-specific instructions.

No Service

Restart your phone and check that the eSIM line is turned on.

Data not working

Some providers require Data Roaming ON for the eSIM line.

Calls or SMS not working

Your plan may be data-only, or passport verification may not be complete.

FAQ

Short answers to common Korea eSIM questions.

Does my phone support eSIM?

Recent iPhone, Samsung Galaxy and Google Pixel models often support eSIM, but always check your exact model and carrier lock status.

Should I buy eSIM before arriving in Korea?

For data-only travel eSIMs, buying before arrival is often convenient. Activation rules depend on provider.

Do I need Wi-Fi to install eSIM?

Yes, use stable Wi-Fi or mobile data to install the eSIM before switching mobile data to the eSIM line.

Can I keep my home SIM card?

On dual SIM supported phones, you can often keep your home SIM active and use the eSIM for Korean data.

Can I use WhatsApp with eSIM?

Yes. WhatsApp, LINE, KakaoTalk and similar apps work with mobile data.

Can I make phone calls with eSIM in Korea?

Only if your plan includes voice calls. Many travel eSIM plans are data-only.

Can I get a Korean phone number with eSIM?

Yes, some Korean eSIM plans may include a Korean number. Check the provider details before buying.

Do I need passport verification?

Data-only eSIM plans generally do not require passport verification. Korean number eSIM plans may require it.

Why does my eSIM show No Service?

The eSIM may not be activated, your phone may not be connected to the correct network, or the eSIM line may be turned off.

Should Data Roaming be ON?

Many travel eSIM providers require Data Roaming ON for the eSIM line. Follow your provider's instructions.

Can I use food delivery apps without a Korean phone number?

Some delivery services may work without a Korean phone number, but having one can make driver communication and local services easier. Requirements change by service and provider.

Before buying: Plan details differ by provider. Confirm data allowance, validity, Korean phone number, calls, SMS, passport verification, hotspot and refund rules.
