Korea mobile setup guide

How to Use eSIM in Korea

Compare data eSIMs, Korean number eSIMs, physical SIM cards, and roaming before you arrive in Korea.

Check phone compatibility Compare options

Start here

Most visitors should first decide whether they only need mobile data or also need a Korean phone number.

  • Phone supports eSIM
  • Phone is not carrier-locked
  • Plan includes the services you need

Fast decision

Quick answer

  • Most short-term travelers can use a data-only eSIM.
  • Travelers who need delivery, local calls, or some reservations may need a Korean phone number.
  • A Korean phone number does not guarantee access to every Korean verification service.
  • Physical SIM cards may be better for longer stays or users who want in-person support.

Compatibility first

Check phone compatibility

Before comparing prices, confirm that your phone can install an eSIM and is not locked to your home carrier. A compatible unlocked phone is the main requirement for using an eSIM in Korea.

What to check

  • Your phone model supports eSIM.
  • Your carrier lock status allows another mobile plan.
  • Your provider supports activation timing that fits your arrival.
  • You can keep your primary SIM active if you need messages from home.

Phone number needs

Do you need a Korean phone number?

Many travelers only need mobile data. A Korean phone number becomes more useful when local services need to call or text you during the trip.

A Korean number may help with

  • Food delivery contact
  • Driver or hotel calls
  • Some reservations
  • SMS messages
  • Longer stays

Important: Having a Korean phone number does not always mean you can complete identity verification on Korean apps.

Verification rules

Passport verification for SIM and eSIM plans

Some Korean telecom or tourist SIM/eSIM products may require passport verification, especially when the plan includes a Korean number, calls, or SMS.

Check before purchase

  • Activation method: QR code, app, counter, or staff setup
  • Pickup method: online activation, airport pickup, delivery, or store visit
  • Passport requirement: upload before travel or verify after arrival
  • Included services: data, Korean number, voice calls, SMS, hotspot

Troubleshooting

Common eSIM problems

QR code does not scan

The code may be expired, already used, or hard to read. Try manual activation details or contact the provider before deleting anything.

eSIM installed but no data

The wrong data line may be selected, data roaming may be off, or activation may not have started. Check the eSIM line settings and provider instructions.

Data works but calls/SMS do not

Your plan may be data-only, or number activation may require verification. Confirm the included services in your plan.

Phone is carrier-locked

A locked phone may not accept another mobile plan. Ask your home carrier about unlocking before you travel.

eSIM was deleted accidentally

Some eSIMs cannot be reinstalled with the same QR code. Contact the provider and ask whether reissue is possible.

eSIM and SIM providers

International eSIM providers

Usually best for data-first travelers who want online purchase and simple setup before arrival.

  • Check whether the plan is data-only.
  • Confirm hotspot, fair usage, speed limits, and refund rules.
  • Do not assume a Korean phone number is included.

Korean eSIM / SIM providers

Usually better when you need local support, a Korean number option, pickup help, or longer stay conditions.

  • Check passport verification and activation timing.
  • Confirm calls, SMS, and Korean number availability.
  • Compare airport pickup, store pickup, and online setup.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I use eSIM immediately after landing in Korea?

Usually yes if your phone supports eSIM, the plan is installed, and the provider allows activation after arrival. Some plans require data roaming to be turned on for the eSIM line.

Do I need passport verification?

Some Korean SIM or eSIM products may require passport verification, especially when the plan includes a Korean phone number, voice calls, or SMS.

Can I get a Korean phone number with eSIM?

Some Korean eSIM plans include a Korean phone number. Many international eSIM plans are data-only, so confirm phone number, calls, and SMS before buying.

Does a Korean phone number work for all Korean app verification?

No. A Korean phone number alone does not guarantee access to every Korean identity verification service. App rules and telecom registration types can differ.

Is Google Maps enough in Korea?

Google Maps can help, but Korea often requires local map apps for better walking, transit, store, and address information. Prepare Naver Map or KakaoMap as alternatives.

Should I buy before arrival or at the airport?

Buying before arrival can save time if your phone is compatible. Airport pickup may be better if you want staff help, a physical SIM, or a Korean number plan.

Can I keep my WhatsApp or LINE number?

Yes. WhatsApp and LINE usually keep using your existing account number as long as the app remains installed and you have mobile data or Wi-Fi.

What happens if I delete my eSIM?

Some eSIMs cannot be reinstalled after deletion. Do not delete an eSIM unless the provider confirms it can be reissued or reinstalled.
