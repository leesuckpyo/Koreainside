How to Use Taxi in Korea

Practical taxi guide for Incheon Airport arrivals, late-night travel, Korean addresses and avoiding unofficial taxi offers.

Quick answer

Use the official taxi stand only. Save your destination address in Korean before leaving the airport.

When should I use a taxi?

Taxi is useful for families, late-night arrivals, heavy luggage, or hotels that are difficult to reach by train or airport bus.

What to do

  1. Follow signs for the official taxi stand.
  2. Do not follow unofficial taxi offers inside the terminal.
  3. Show your destination in Korean.
  4. Keep your phone map open during the ride if possible.

Common mistakes

  • Showing only an English hotel name.
  • Accepting unofficial taxi offers.
  • Not preparing a backup payment method.

FAQ

Do taxi drivers speak English?

Some do, but many do not. Korean address screenshots are very helpful.

Can I pay by foreign card?

Often yes, but prepare cash or another card as backup.

Should I use Kakao T?

Kakao T can be useful later in your trip, but airport official taxi stands are easier for first arrival.
