Accommodation insights

How travelers compare stay types

Use this section to understand trade-offs before adding hotel-level partner integrations.

Hotel

Best for first-time visitors who want predictable service, front desk support and easy booking conditions.

Budget Hotel

Best for travelers balancing privacy, location and price without accepting hostel-style trade-offs.

Apartment

Best for longer stays, couples or travelers who value laundry, extra space and neighborhood routines.

Serviced Apartment

Best for families, business stays and travelers who want apartment comfort with hotel-like reliability.

Hanok Stay

Best for experience-led travelers who accept limited space, older buildings or stricter house rules.

Hostel

Best for solo and budget travelers who prioritize price, social energy and central access.
